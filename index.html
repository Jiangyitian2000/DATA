Showcasing Singapore's Parks for Tourist Attraction

Garden City Singapore is a data visualization project that showcases the tourism appeal of Singaporeâ€™s parks and highlights visitor distribution patterns.

The storymap utilizes Mapbox to display over 300 parks, from the iconic Gardens by the Bay to the serene Singapore Botanic Gardens. It reveals tourist density disparities, where hotspots like Marina Bay Sands attract millions while lesser-known green spaces remain under-visited.

Through the crowdsourced map, users can contribute insights on hidden gems like Punggol Park or suggest improvements to enhance park accessibility and visitor experience via the Ushahidi platform.
