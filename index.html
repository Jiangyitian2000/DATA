Food deserts in Singapore

Food deserts in Singapore is a data visualziation project that shows neighborhoods in Singapore that might suffer from access to fresh food options.

The storymap utilizes government open data that displays the location of supermarkets and hawker centers as a source of fresh food and calculates the walkshed of access to fresh food locations as 500 meters of network distance from all HDB flats in Sinagpore.

Through the crowdsourced map, users can report alternative sources of fresh food through the ushahidi platform.
